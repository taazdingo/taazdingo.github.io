The Sun has a son and he's ironically photosensitive! He'll burn in his father's wake.

Guide the Sun into manipulating shadows so that his son can reach the other side without burning up

Son of a Sun is a class project out of a Game Mechanics class. The given theme was 'Sun' and we were required to design mechanics. This was our take on it. The idea was to use the shadows as game elements and create a puzzle.

Controls:

Left and Right arrow keys to move the Sun

Spacebar to move the son two steps at a time
